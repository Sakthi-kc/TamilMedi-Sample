Turmeric

Make a paste with turmeric powder and water and apply on the skin. Once dried, rinse off and pat dry. You can replace water with honey for dry skin.

Potato

Slice thin disks from a potato and massage your face gently with it.

Papaya

Wash your face, pat dry and apply papaya paste on your face. Leave the mask on for 20-40 minutes and rinse off with warm water. Repeat daily.

Lemon

Apply lemon juice directly to the pimple overnight. It not only cures pimple but protects your skin too.

Neem leaves

Apply neem paste on the affected area, leave it dry, rinse with water and pat dry. Make neem leaf paste by boiling and crushing 10 leaves.
